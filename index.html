Welcome to Anime World!

The Big Three is a term used to describe the three most popular running series during their golden age in Jump's mid 2000s period - One Piece, Naruto and Bleach.

The Big-3 Anime:-

One Piece

One Piece is an epic tale about Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they embark on a thrilling adventure to find the legendary treasure, One Piece, and become the Pirate King.

Bleach

Bleach follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager with the ability to see ghosts, as he gains the powers of a Soul Reaper and fights to protect both the human world and the afterlife from evil spirits.

Naruto

Naruto tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a dream of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the title of Hokage, all while battling powerful enemies and seeking acceptance from his village.
